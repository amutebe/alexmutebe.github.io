e-Portfolio Preparation

As part of the research methods and professional practice module, I was tasked with creating an e-Portfolio. This assignment required me to compile evidence of my work throughout the module, including reflective activities, statistics exercises, and showcasing at least one artifact from each unit. Initially intrigued by the task, I grappled with selecting the right artifacts and articulating my reflections clearly. However, guided by Gibbs’ Reflective Cycle, I systematically evaluated my experiences and improved my work. This process enhanced my reflective skills and underscored the significance of organized presentation. Looking ahead, I’ll continue using reflective models, seek feedback, and explore advanced e-Portfolio techniques. Ultimately, this reflective exercise has prepared me for professional challenges by emphasizing continuous improvement and thoughtful self-assessment.
