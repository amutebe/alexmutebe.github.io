Quantitative Methods - Descriptive and Inferential Statistics

As an aspiring data scientist, this module allowed me to explore the fundamental concepts that underpin data analysis. I will delve into my experiences, emotions, and newfound insights. The module commenced with an exploration of descriptive statistics. These statistical tools provide a snapshot of data, summarizing its central tendencies and variability. I learned about measures of location (such as mean, median, and mode) and measures of spread (such as variance and standard deviation). Additionally, we discussed the different levels of measurement—nominal, ordinal, interval, and ratio—which influence the choice of statistical techniques. Initially, I felt a mix of curiosity and apprehension. The world of statistics seemed vast, and I wondered how these abstract concepts would translate into practical applications. However, as I engaged with the material, my interest grew. I appreciated the power of descriptive statistics in revealing patterns and trends hidden within data. This module illuminated the power of statistics in unravelling patterns and making informed decisions. I now appreciate the delicate balance between descriptive and inferential approaches. As I move forward, I’ll continue honing my statistical skills, exploring hypothesis testing, and diving deeper into data visualisation.
