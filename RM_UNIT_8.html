Data Analysis and Visualisation

Unit 8, focusing on data analysis and presentation e.g. the hypothesis testing exercises, where we compared mean incomes between males and females using a t-test. The p-value (0.00078) was significantly lower than 0.05, leading us to reject the null hypothesis. This provided strong evidence that the population mean income for males exceeds that of females. This practical application of statistical analysis was challenging but rewarding, and it highlighted the importance of accurate data interpretation (Shao et al., 2022). The unit was well-structured, balancing theory and practice. Using excel for data analysis was particularly insightful. In my data management role, these skills are invaluable.

Reference:

Shao, C., Yang, Y., Juneja, S. and GSeetharam, T., 2022. IoT data visualization for business intelligence in corporate finance. Information Processing & Management, 59(1), p.102736.
