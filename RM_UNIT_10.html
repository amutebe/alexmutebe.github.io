Research Writing

I embarked on creating an audio-narrated slide presentation and a detailed transcript. The chosen topic focused on enhancing epidemic modelling using machine learning. I carefully structured the content to align with the requirements of a standard research proposal for the Computing department. Initially, I felt both excited and challenged by the task. The blend of technical content and effective communication intrigued me. The audio-narrated slides allowed me to convey complex ideas visually. Crafting the transcript required clarity, precision, and coherence. I recognized the importance of tailoring the message for different audiences. Epidemic modelling and machine learning intersected seamlessly. I considered the impact of my words and visuals on audience understanding. I learned that effective research communication demands thoughtful adaptation. The synthesis of content, visuals, and narration is an art. In the future, the plan is to apply these insights to my future presentations and final research proposal.
