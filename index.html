Alex Mutebe, the data scientist

Biography,

Alex Mutebe, MSc, is a Senior Data Scientist at the MRC/UVRI & London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine Uganda Research Unit. He brings over a decade of experience in data science, epidemiology, and clinical research, with a focus on applying advanced analytics and machine learning to pressing global health challenges. At MRC/UVRI & LSHTM, Alex leads data-driven projects that support infectious disease research, vaccine studies, and health systems strengthening. His expertise spans building robust data pipelines, predictive modelling, and reproducible workflows, alongside developing dashboards and visual tools that translate complex data into actionable insights. Passionate about responsible AI, Alex continues to explore innovative applications of machine learning in healthcare while contributing to the growth of Africa’s AI research community.
