Introduction to Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs)

During the module we covered the fundamental principles and applications of ANNs, inspired by biological neural networks. We studied the architecture of neurons and layers, learning processes like backpropagation, and various network types including feedforward, convolutional (CNNs), and recurrent (RNNs) networks. Practical sessions involved implementing perceptron and operator, building and training models using python and jupyter notebooks on image recognition. The ethical discussions highlighted the need for responsible AI development to avoid biases and ensure fairness.

This module deepened my understanding of ANNs and their role in the Industry 4.0 revolution.I learned about training Artificial Neural Networks (ANN) and the backpropagation algorithm.

Armed with this knowledge, I plan to pursue advanced studies in neural network architectures and applications, particularly in impactful fields like healthcare. I will engage in continuous learning through courses, workshops, and AI community involvement. Committed to ethical AI, I will prioritize fairness and transparency in my future work, aiming to contribute positively to society with responsible AI solutions.
